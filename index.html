About Me

I am a Senior Engineer from the PolarDB Storage Engine Team, Alibaba Cloud. I joined Alibaba after I obtained my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from Nanyang Technological University, Singapore. Before that, I received my Bachelor degree in Computer Science and Technology from Northwestern Polytechnical University, China.

At PolarDB, I take a focus on the research of cloud-native database technologies as well as their applications in the cloud market. In particular, I have been working on X-Engine, a LSM-tree based storage engine for MySQL, the HTAP execution engine of PolarDB, and other related systems/projects. Before joining Alibaba, I conducted my Ph.D. research in the field of high-performance main-memory databases, especially the designs, optimizations and implementations of main-memory database algorithms using emerging hardware such as the Intel Xeon Phi many-core processor, the High-Bandwidth Memory (a.k.a., Multi-Channel DRAMs), GPGPUs, etc.

My team is continuously offering internship opportunities for students interested in the making of system software or databases.

Research Interests

I am interested in formulating research problems from real-world scenarios of databases in the cloud, exploring solutions to real technical challenges, and building practical systems that are valuable to customers:

  • Cloud-Native and Distributed Database Architectures
  • Main-Memory Data Storage and Indexing
  • Cloud Storage for Databases
  • Hybrid Transactional/Analytical Processing
